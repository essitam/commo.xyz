SUBMIT



Submissions are currently CLOSED. Please check back soon!

Submit: Submit up to ten (10) pages of writing, whatever form it takes i.e. poetry, fiction, non-fiction, interview, essay, translation, script, etc. However, submissions need not fit within any genre. Include a short bio and anything else you would like us to know about your work. If there is accompanying media you would like to include with your submission, pass it along. Please send your submission as an attachment, preferably word doc (.doc) although PDFs are fine for unconventionally formatted works. If submitting multiple pieces of writing, include them in a single document with each piece beginning on a new page. The subject line of your email should be SUBMISSION.

We encourage everyone to submit, regardless of publication history. Writers living on the margins take priority.

All published work remains the intellectual property of the artist. We prefer work that is previously unpublished. Simultaneous submissions are fine, but let us know if your work has been accepted elsewhere.

Expect to hear back from us within one month of closing submissions. If after that point you have not heard from us, please send a follow-up inquiry.
